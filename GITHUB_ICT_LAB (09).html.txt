    <h1>Introduction</h1>
    <p>
        Air University (AU) is a public-sector university established by the Pakistan Air Force (PAF).
        It is one of Pakistan’s leading institutions known for high-quality education, advanced research,
        and disciplined academic culture.
    </p>

    <h2>Vision and Mission</h2>
    <h3>Vision</h3>
    <p>
        To be a leading national university contributing to the country’s progress through innovation,
        research, and quality education.
    </p>

    <h3>Mission</h3>
    <p>
        To produce graduates who are technically competent, ethically strong, and capable of contributing
        to national development.
    </p>

    <h2>Campuses</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>
            <strong>Islamabad Campus:</strong> Main campus offering engineering, computing, management,
            and social sciences.
        </li>
        <li>
            <strong>Kamra Campus (Aerospace & Aviation):</strong> Specializes in aerospace, avionics,
            and engineering programs.
        </li>
        <li>
            <strong>Multan Campus:</strong> Offers programs in engineering, business, computing,
            and health sciences.
        </li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Achievements</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>High ranking among engineering and IT universities</li>
        <li>Strong alumni network</li>
        <li>National contributions in aerospace and defense research</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Conclusion</h2>
    <p>
        Air University is a reputable institution that provides high-quality education, modern research
        opportunities, and a disciplined learning environment. With strong ties to the Pakistan Air Force,
        it continues to play a major role in producing skilled professionals for Pakistan’s academic,
        industrial, and defense sectors.
    </p>
